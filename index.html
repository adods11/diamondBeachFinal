About the Condo

Diamond Beach is a beautiful five star resort with amenities unmatched by any other Galveston destination. Our spectacular home is on the sixth floor with a large balcony, offering a bird's-eye view of West Beach and the Gulf. It is the only property on the seawall that has direct access to the beach without having to cross Seawall Boulevard.
